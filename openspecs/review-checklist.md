# Review Checklist

## Content QA
- Thong diep co huong business value?
- Co metric cu the va de tin?
- Co CTA ro rang o dau va cuoi trang?

## UX QA
- Hero co ro value proposition trong 5 giay dau?
- Section flow co logic challenge -> solution -> proof -> CTA?
- Responsive mobile co de doc?

## Technical QA
- Build pass cho en/vi.
- Khong co broken links.
- Navbar/footer khong tro den route da tat.

## Governance QA
- Legal disclaimer con hop le?
- Khong lo thong tin nhay cam.
- Quyết dinh moi da duoc ghi vao decisions.
