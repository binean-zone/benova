The Challenge: Legacy Insurance Systems

Understanding where you are today is the first step to reaching where you need to be.

The System That Built Your Business​

Ingenium is the insurance core system that has powered your operations for years — managing everything from policy intake and underwriting to claims processing and payments. It is stable, proven, and deeply embedded in your business processes.

But stability comes at a cost.

What Your Team Deals With Every Day​

Aging Technology Stack​

  • COBOL on AIX — a powerful combination in its time, but today's talent pool is shrinking rapidly
  • IBM MQ and WebSphere — expensive middleware that is no longer fully supported
  • On-premise only — rigid infrastructure that cannot flex with business demand

Operational Bottlenecks​

  • Manual deployments that take hours and carry significant risk
  • No modern APIs — integration with digital services requires custom middleware for every connection
  • Copy-paste operations between environments — the root cause of countless production incidents

Rising Costs​

  • Specialized skills becoming scarce — COBOL developers are retiring, replacements are rare
  • Proprietary licensing — AIX, MQ, WebSphere, and Db2 licensing costs continue to rise
  • Manual processes require dedicated staff for tasks that should be automated

Compliance and Security Gaps​

  • Credentials scattered across config files — an audit finding waiting to happen
  • No centralized monitoring — incidents are discovered when customers complain
  • Manual change management — error-prone processes that create compliance exposure

The Business Impact​

These are not just technical inconveniences. They translate directly to:

AreaImpact
RevenueSlow time-to-market for new insurance products loses competitive opportunities
CostsLegacy maintenance consumes budget that could fund innovation
Customer experienceOutdated interfaces and slow processing drives customers to competitors
RiskManual processes create security and compliance exposure
TalentDifficulty recruiting developers willing to work with legacy technology

The Transformation Imperative​

The question is no longer whether to modernize — it is how to modernize safely.

Your core system contains decades of business logic, millions of customer records, and processes that are critical to your operation. A reckless approach could be catastrophic. But continuing to invest in legacy infrastructure is equally unsustainable.

What You Need​

  • A transformation approach that protects your current operations while building the future
  • Immediate wins that justify the investment and build organizational momentum
  • A platform that is purpose-built for insurance — not a generic cloud migration tool
  • A partner that has done this before and understands the unique challenges of Ingenium

This Is Exactly Why Nova Exists​

Binean Nova was created specifically to address these challenges — delivering a proven, phased approach to modernization that works alongside your existing system.
