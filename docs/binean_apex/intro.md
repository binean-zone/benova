# Apex — The Summit of Innovation

> **A completely modern insurance platform — no compromises.**

---

## What Apex Delivers

Apex represents the ultimate vision of the Nova transformation — a fully re-architected insurance core:

- **Complete Rust Core** — All business logic migrated from COBOL to high-performance, memory-safe Rust
- **Modern Data Architecture** — Cloud-native distributed databases replacing legacy Db2
- **Unlimited Integration** — Open APIs and event-driven architecture connecting to any service
- **Dynamic Ecosystem** — A platform that evolves continuously with your business needs
